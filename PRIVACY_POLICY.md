# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-07

This Privacy Policy describes how "Real Ghost Ball" ("the App") handles information.

## Summary
- The App uses the device camera to provide augmented-reality visuals.
- Processing happens on the device. The App does not send camera data off the device.
- The App does not collect, store, or share personal information.

## Information We Collect
We do not collect personal information. The App does not require account creation and does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or identifiers.

## Camera and Sensor Data
The App accesses the camera and device sensors (e.g., LiDAR, motion) to detect pool balls and render AR overlays. This data is processed in real time on the device and is not stored or transmitted.

## Analytics and Third-Party Services
The App does not use analytics SDKs or third-party tracking services.

## Data Sharing
We do not share data with third parties.

## Data Retention
No user data is stored. Camera and sensor data are processed in memory only.
